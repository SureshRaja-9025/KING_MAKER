__ ABOUT US:

EMPOWERING INNOVATION

3xper is a young and nimble custom research, contract development, and manufacturing organization (CDMO). 3xper stands for Experience, Expertise, and Experiments. We strive to provide best-in-class service offerings in CDMO with a focus on innovation. Our offerings are spread across discovery services, development services, clinical batch manufacturing, and commercial manufacturing services. Our services are offered to global innovator customers in pharma, biotech, specialty, nutraceutical, and animal health companies. 3xper embraces a customer-centric culture, placing the needs and aspirations of our customers at the heart of everything we do, and we intend to expedite our customer’s innovation.Read more
